About

The Man Behind the Ministry

Not a guru. Not a celebrity pastor. Just a Marine who found Jesus, fought for his family, and decided to help other men do the same.

Adam Johns

Christ-Following Husband. Father. Minister. Marine.

Adam Johns is a Christ-following husband, father, minister, and Marine — a 21-year United States Marine Corps veteran, combat veteran, military pilot, ordained minister (CLA/CLI & NACM), and NASM-certified coach running five businesses while raising three children with his wife Maria in Fredericksburg, Virginia.

After serving in the Marines, Adam felt called to help men navigate the same battles he faced — strengthening marriages, raising children with purpose, building businesses with integrity, and walking with Christ in everything.

U.S.M.C. Ministries was born from a simple conviction: men need brothers, not just advice. The name stands for what we do — Uniting, Serving, Mentoring, Counseling — and every program reflects those pillars.

Adam's approach is unapologetically biblical, practically military, and deeply personal. He doesn't coach from a distance — he's in the trenches with you.

Our Mission

What USMC Stands For

U

Uniting

Brotherhood Formation — Bringing men together in biblical fellowship. Iron sharpens iron. No man was designed to fight alone.

S

Serving

Community Outreach — Putting faith into action through service. The church isn't a building — it's a mission. We go where the need is.

M

Mentoring

One-on-One Discipleship — Walking alongside men as they grow in faith, lead their families, and step into their calling. Titus 2 in action.

C

Counseling

Pastoral Care — Pre/post-marriage counseling, life guidance, and confidential pastoral care. The hard conversations men need but rarely get.
