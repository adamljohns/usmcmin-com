AI Tools Landscape

Thirty tools across seven categories. What each one does. What it costs. Where it overlaps with something you might already have. Most captains need 2–4 of these — not all 30. Skip to Captain’s Stacks if you just want a recommendation.

30 tools7 categories3 recommended stacks
Pricing reflects publicly listed figures from mid-2025. Verify before subscribing — AI vendors change tiers monthly. Free tiers also shrink without warning.

The 30 tools

Tap a chip above to filter. Cards group by category in the default view.

Chat

Claude

Anthropic's general AI assistant.

Strong at writing, reasoning, and code. Long context window. Cowork mode runs agentic workflows on your desktop / mobile — the differentiator most other chat apps don't have.
Free tier · Pro $20/mo · Max from $100/mo
Best default for most men. If you only buy one, this is the one.
Chat

ChatGPT

OpenAI's assistant. Largest install base.

GPT-5 family models, image generation built into the chat, custom GPTs marketplace, voice mode. The default for many guys who tried AI first.
Free tier · Plus $20/mo · Pro $200/mo · Business / Enterprise scales up
Redundancy check — Heavily overlaps Claude for general writing & reasoning. Pick one as primary; second is optional cross-check.
Worth it as a #2 for image gen and a sanity check. Skip if you have Claude and don't generate images.
Chat

Gemini

Google's AI, baked into Workspace.

Strong free tier and the longest context window of the big three. Best when you live in Google Drive, Docs, Gmail — it sees your stuff with permission.
Free tier · AI Pro $19.99/mo (often included in Google One)
Redundancy check — Overlaps Claude / ChatGPT. Becomes essential only if your work is Google-native.
Skip unless you're deep in Google. Free tier is decent for the curious.
Chat

Grok

xAI's chat, built into X (Twitter).

Best for live X data and an unfiltered conversational style. Limited utility outside that lane for most captains.
Bundled with X Premium $8/mo+ · Standalone Super Grok ~$30/mo
Redundancy check — Heavily redundant with ChatGPT / Claude for general work.
Skip unless you're a heavy X user or want a contrarian second take. Don't pay for it as a primary.
Chat

Perplexity

Search-first AI. Citations built in.

Real-time web search wrapped in chat with proper citations. Replaces "Google for research" for most captains.
Free tier · Pro $20/mo
Redundancy check — Overlaps ChatGPT / Gemini search modes. Standalone is cleaner if research is your bottleneck.
Free tier is enough for 90%. Pro only if you research daily for work.
Chat

Microsoft Copilot

Microsoft's AI in Edge, Windows, M365.

Integrated across Windows, Edge, and Microsoft 365 apps. The right pick if you live in Outlook / Word / Excel and your IT shop is Microsoft.
Free tier · Copilot Pro $20/mo · M365 Copilot $30/mo per user
Redundancy check — Redundant with Claude / ChatGPT for chat. Earned its keep when paired with M365.
Pay only if your daily work is in Word / Excel / Outlook. Otherwise skip.
Voice

OpenAI Whisper

Industry-leading transcription.

Open source model with leading accuracy across accents and noise. Available via OpenAI API ($0.006/min) or local apps (MacWhisper, etc.). Most other transcription tools use Whisper under the hood.
Free (local) · ~$0.006/min via OpenAI API
Redundancy check — If your iPhone already transcribes Voice Memos to your taste, you may not need Whisper.
Run locally on a Mac if you have one. Otherwise let Apple or Otter handle it.
Voice

Apple Voice Memos

Built-in iPhone transcription.

Voice Memos transcribes your recordings on-device on iOS 18+ with Apple Intelligence. Apple Notes records audio with live transcripts too. May be all you need.
Free (with iOS 18+ on a recent iPhone)
Redundancy check — Replaces Otter and most paid transcription tools for personal use.
Try this first. If it covers your captures, you're done.
Voice

Otter.ai

Live meeting transcription, speaker ID.

Joins Zoom / Google Meet / Teams calls and transcribes in real time with speakers labeled. Searchable archive of meetings is the killer feature.
Free tier (300 min/mo) · Pro $16.99/mo · Business $30/mo per user
Redundancy check — If your meeting platform already records with transcripts (Zoom AI Companion, Google Meet Gemini), you may not need Otter.
Worth it if you have 3+ meetings/week you wish you'd captured. Otherwise skip.
Voice

Descript

Edit audio & video by editing the transcript.

Type to delete words, type to overdub, AI fills in gaps. Strong for podcasters and short-video creators. Word-level editing of audio is genuinely magic the first time.
Free tier · Hobbyist $19/mo · Creator $35/mo
Redundancy check — Overlaps with traditional editors (Premiere, Final Cut) for video; the transcript-edit workflow is unique.
Buy it the month you start a podcast or YouTube channel. Skip otherwise.
Image

Midjourney

Best aesthetic quality for stylized images.

Web app or Discord-based image generation. Strongest house style of any image model — gets you to publishable visuals fastest if your taste matches its aesthetic.
Basic $10/mo · Standard $30/mo · Pro $60/mo
Redundancy check — Replaces DALL-E / Firefly for most creative use. Less integrated with workflows.
Worth it if you make weekly visual content. Skip if image gen is occasional — ChatGPT's built-in is enough.
Image

DALL-E (in ChatGPT)

Image gen baked into ChatGPT.

GPT-Image-1 / DALL-E inside ChatGPT. Convenient because it's right where you're already chatting. Quality is good, not best-in-class.
Included with ChatGPT Plus $20/mo
Redundancy check — Redundant with Midjourney / Firefly if you have either.
The right pick if you only generate the occasional image. Don't pay for ChatGPT just for this.
Image

Adobe Firefly

Commercially safe, trained on licensed content.

Firefly is trained on Adobe Stock + licensed content, so output is safer for commercial use. Lives inside Photoshop / Illustrator if you have Creative Cloud.
Standalone from $4.99/mo · Included with Creative Cloud subscriptions
Redundancy check — Pricing-wise hard to beat if you already pay for CC. Standalone is duplicate of Midjourney.
Use it when commercial-safety matters (logos, marketing, products you're selling).
Image

Ideogram

Best for text-in-image (logos, posters).

The image model that actually spells words right. Strong for logos, posters, social cards with copy on them — the place other image models fall apart.
Free tier (limited) · Plus $7/mo · Pro $20/mo
Redundancy check — Specialty tool. Doesn't replace a general image model.
$7 stack-add for the captain who makes flyers, sermon graphics, or marketing posts.
Image

Stable Diffusion

Open source. Free if you self-host.

The open foundation model behind many image apps. Run locally (free, requires a decent GPU) or via paid hosted services.
Free (self-hosted) · Hosted services from $0–$30/mo
Redundancy check — Niche unless you need full control or local privacy.
Skip unless you're technical and want to own the stack. Most captains are better served by Midjourney or Firefly.
Notes

Notion AI

AI inside the Notion docs you already use.

Q&A across your workspace, draft / summarize / translate inline, AI databases. Best if Notion is already your second brain.
Notion Free / Plus $10 + AI add-on $10/mo · Business $24+/mo includes AI
Redundancy check — Overlaps Apple Notes + Apple Intelligence and Obsidian + plugins for personal use.
Add the AI on if Notion is your daily driver. Skip if you only open Notion monthly.
Notes

Obsidian + AI plugins

Local-first knowledge graph.

Markdown files on your hard drive, linked into a graph. Pair with Smart Connections / Copilot plugin for AI search across your notes — without sending them to the cloud.
Obsidian free for personal · Some plugins free, some paid
Redundancy check — Replaces Notion for the privacy-conscious captain. Different model than database apps.
For the captain who wants files on disk, owned, portable. Curve is steeper than Notion's.
Notes

Apple Notes

Built-in. Now AI-aware.

With Apple Intelligence: summarize notes, audio recordings with transcripts, writing tools. Already on your phone, already syncs to your Mac.
Free (with iOS / macOS 18+ on a recent device)
Redundancy check — Replaces a lot of paid note tools for personal use.
Try this first. Move to Notion / Obsidian only if you hit a wall.
Notes

Granola

AI meeting notes that listen to your audio context.

Mac-only. Listens to your Zoom / Meet / Teams audio, generates structured meeting notes. Different from Otter — less raw transcript, more clean notes.
Free tier · Pro $18/mo · Business $35/mo per user
Redundancy check — Otter.ai is the live alternative. Granola feels less like a recorder, more like a notetaker.
Worth it if your day is back-to-back calls. Free tier is enough to test.
Automation

Anthropic Cowork

Desktop / mobile agent built into Claude.

Run files, automate browser tasks, execute multi-step workflows on your machine with the captain still on the bridge. The bootcamp's primary tool.
Included with Claude Pro / Max (Pro $20/mo, Max from $100/mo)
Redundancy check — Replaces basic Zapier for personal workflows. Doesn't replace Zapier for multi-app SaaS chains.
This is the differentiator. The reason Claude is the bootcamp's default.
Automation

Zapier

No-code automation between apps.

The granddaddy of automation. 7,000+ app integrations. AI features now baked across the product. Free tier handles light use.
Free tier (5 single-step Zaps) · Paid from $19.99/mo
Redundancy check — Overlaps Make.com (Make is more flexible / cheaper at scale).
Free tier is fine for the captain with 1–2 simple automations. Pay only if you have a real workflow.
Automation

Make.com

More flexible than Zapier. Visual.

Branching, looping, complex visual workflows. Better unit economics than Zapier above the free tier. Steeper learning curve.
Free tier (1,000 ops/mo) · Paid from $9/mo
Redundancy check — Functional overlap with Zapier — pick one.
Pick this over Zapier if your automations branch or loop. Otherwise Zapier's marketplace is bigger.
Automation

n8n

Open source automation. Self-hosted option.

Same shape as Zapier / Make but you can self-host for full control. Strong AI nodes. The captain's choice for owning the stack.
Self-host free · Cloud from $20/mo · Pro $50/mo
Redundancy check — Replaces Zapier / Make for the technical captain.
Skip unless you're already running a homelab or care about data sovereignty.
Coding

Claude Code

Anthropic's terminal coding agent.

Run from your terminal. Reads your repo, writes / edits code, runs tests, opens PRs — under your supervision. Feels like a senior engineer pair-programming.
Included with Claude Pro / Max
Redundancy check — Different shape than Cursor (terminal vs editor). Many devs run both.
For captains shipping real code — this is the bootcamp's recommendation.
Coding

Cursor

AI-first code editor. Fork of VS Code.

Tab autocomplete that's eerily good. Composer / Agent for multi-file edits. Industry standard for AI-assisted coding in 2025–26.
Free tier (limited) · Pro $20/mo · Business $40/mo per user
Redundancy check — Different shape than Claude Code. Comparable to Windsurf.
Pick this if you live in a graphical editor; Claude Code if you live in a terminal.
Coding

GitHub Copilot

Tight GitHub / VS Code integration.

Autocomplete, chat, PR summaries inside GitHub and VS Code. Now multi-model (Claude, GPT, Gemini available). Free tier for individuals.
Free tier (limited) · Pro $10/mo · Business $19/mo per user
Redundancy check — Cursor / Windsurf are stronger AI editors; Copilot wins on GitHub integration.
Worth $10 if your team is on GitHub. Otherwise Cursor's ergonomics win.
Coding

Windsurf

Cursor competitor. Strong agent mode.

Codeium's AI editor — same idea as Cursor with a different feel. Strong agent / cascade mode. Free tier is generous.
Free tier · Pro from $15/mo
Redundancy check — Direct competitor to Cursor. Pick one.
Worth a 1-week trial alongside Cursor; whichever feels more natural is the winner.
Video

ElevenLabs

Industry-leading AI voice / TTS.

Voice cloning, narration, multilingual dubbing. The voices on most polished AI-narrated content come from here.
Free tier (limited) · Starter $5/mo · Creator $22/mo · Pro $99/mo
Redundancy check — Different shape than Whisper (TTS vs STT).
For the captain producing audiobooks, narrated devotionals, or polished video voiceovers.
Video

HeyGen

AI avatars. Talking-head video without filming.

Generate talking-head video from a script and a photo / clone of yourself. Real-time avatars in some plans. Strong for course / training content at scale.
Free tier (1 min) · Creator $24/mo · Team $89/mo per user
Redundancy check — Niche — replaces filming, not editing.
Skip for personal cohort use (real face matters). Worth it for high-volume course / sales content.
Video

OpusClip

Auto-clip long videos into shorts.

Drop in a long video, get back ~10 short-form clips with captions, cropping, hooks. Saves hours per upload if you publish to TikTok / Reels / Shorts.
Free tier (60 min/mo) · Starter $9/mo · Pro $29/mo
Redundancy check — Descript can do similar with more manual work.
Worth $9 if you publish weekly to short-form social. Skip otherwise.

Captain’s Stacks

You don’t need all 30. Pick the stack that fits your season — and don’t pay for the next tier until the current one is the bottleneck.

MINIMUM VIABLE

$0 / mo

For the captain who just wants to start

  • Claude — free tier, daily message limits
  • Apple Voice Memos / Notes — built into iPhone (iOS 18+)
  • Perplexity — free, replaces "Google for research"
  • Apple Intelligence — free image/writing tools on a recent iPhone
For: men who want to see what AI does before they pay for anything.
SOLO FOUNDER

~$40–60 / mo

For the captain running a business or ministry

  • Claude Pro $20 — primary AI
  • ChatGPT Plus $20 — image gen + alt model
  • Otter.ai Pro $17 — meeting transcription
  • Optional: Zapier free or $20 starter
For: most men in the bootcamp. The 80/20 stack.
POWER USER

~$150–200 / mo

For the captain leveraging AI heavily

  • Claude Max $100 — Cowork-heavy
  • ChatGPT Plus $20 — alt
  • Midjourney Standard $30 — visual
  • ElevenLabs Creator $22 — voice
  • Descript Creator $35 — podcast/video
For: captains who already ship 5+ hrs/wk of content.
