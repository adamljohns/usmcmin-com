USMC Ministries AI Mission

5 weeks. 15 tasks. Each Watchman with Christ at the center.

Disciplined, Christ-centered AI use for husbands, fathers, and leaders. Claude and Cowork primary. AI on the bridge as a trusted aide — not the autopilot. Built for men leading at home, in business, and in ministry.

Format: 3 tasks/week × 5 weeks Time: 20–30 min per task Cohort: Private Telegram channel
Join the Telegram channel Preview the path ↓
The Mission

Christ at the center. The watchman in command.

This isn't a chatbot mission. It's leadership formation. We teach men how to use AI without losing themselves to it. Every week opens and closes in Scripture. AI drafts. The watchman decides.

At Home

Husband, father, brother. Tools serve the home; they never compete with it.

In Business

Solo founder, steward of time. Leverage as a tithe of time, not a brag.

In Ministry

Watchman, servant, multiplier. Train the next watchman (2 Tim 2:2).

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and lean not on your own understanding (or your tools)." — Proverbs 3:5–6 ↗
How It Works

From your phone. No second app to install.

Most men already have Telegram. If you don't, it's a free 60-second install. The whole mission runs in a private channel — no Mighty Networks, no Discord, no Slack, no tier-3 SaaS to learn.

STEP 1

Join the channel

One tap. t.me invite link. Private, free, no email gate.

STEP 2

Read the pinned index

The mission map is the pinned message. Materials and W1.T1 are pinned right behind it.

STEP 3

Ship a task

20-30 minutes. Open the PDF, do the action, react ✓ on the post when shipped.

STEP 4

Drop a one-liner

Tap Comments — Telegram auto-routes you to the linked discussion group. Iron sharpens iron.

The Path

Five weeks. One watchman.

Each week drops three tasks. Each task ships an artifact. By Week 5, every watchman leaves with a signed Watchman's Charter and a person they've taught.

Optional depth track: 35 ninety-second Watchman's Drills run alongside the main tasks. Skip without guilt — the drill is the rep, not the workout.

No second app to install. No funnel. Just the channel link, the mission index pinned, and the watchman's actions stacking.

What "shipped" means

You did the task. Imperfect actions count. Watchman's actions stack.

Heartbeat posts in the cohort feed celebrate what shipped, name what slipped — never grade.
