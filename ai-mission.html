Common Questions

What men ask before they enroll.

If your question isn’t here, email Adam. He answers every one.

Do I need to be technical?
No. The course assumes operator-level common sense — not a CS degree. If you can drive a smartphone and write a paragraph, you can run every task. The technical depth lives in Week 11 (Sovereign Stack) and that week is graded for the watchman who's invested in local AI; if you're not there, you'll still ship the cloud workflow and skip the local install without falling behind.
How much time will this take per week?
Three tasks a week. 20–30 minutes per task. Plus optional daily drills (90 seconds each). Total: ~90 minutes/week of structured work plus however much you decide to spend applying what you learn. We've held this pace with USMC Ministries for over a year — it's the most sustainable rhythm we've found for working husbands and fathers.
Is this just for veterans?
No. The watchman framing comes from Ezekiel 33 — the man set on the wall to warn the city. Veterans recognize the posture viscerally; the cohort is full of solo founders, ministry leaders, pastors, and working dads alongside men who served. The common denominator is the Watchman's Posture, not a DD-214.
What if I can't make a live call?
There are no required live calls. The cohort runs in a Telegram channel — tasks drop Monday / Wednesday / Friday and you ship them on your own schedule. Optional Q&A threads run weekly; you read and contribute when it fits.
What's the difference between this and the free public content?
The free pages (Toolkit, Mission, Sovereign Stack, Heartbeat) are the reference library — what tools exist, how they're tiered, what the program covers. The paid cohort is the doing: 36 specific tasks with deadlines, a private cohort to ship in, and the personal accountability of a 12-week commitment.
What if I want a refund?
Email Adam in the first 14 days, explain why, and you get a full refund — no friction, no questions. We'd rather you walk away with your $199 than carry resentment about a program that didn't fit. After 14 days the access is yours for life.
Why $199 vs $299?
$199 is the intro price for the next cohort. After that cohort kicks off, the standard price moves to $299 (which is itself well below the $1k–$3k men's cohort programs in this space). If you've been on the fence, the intro window is the easiest yes.
Will my data leave my country / be sold / train a model?
Cohort discussion stays on Telegram (their terms apply). Your specific prompts, family details, and pastoral notes never go in the cohort feed — Week 11 (Sovereign Stack) is literally the answer: here's how to wire local AI for the things that shouldn't leave your machine. The course teaches the boundary; the watchman enforces it.
