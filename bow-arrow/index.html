Psalm 127:4–5

"Like arrows in the hand of a warrior, so are the children of one's youth."

Bow & Arrow takes its name from that psalm — the one that keeps our household honest about why we work. Our three children are the arrows; the studios, the properties, and the ministry are simply the bow. Every stay booked, every portrait commissioned, every dollar that flows through this business funds another year of homeschool, another missions trip, another tuition payment, and another shift for U.S.M.C. Ministries.

If you stay with us or hire Maria, you're not just transacting — you're resourcing a household trying to raise three arrows straight.
