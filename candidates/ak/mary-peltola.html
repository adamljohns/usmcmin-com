Official Profile

Religion: Russian Orthodox (Orthodox Church in Alaska — sizeable AK Native demographic via 19th-c. Russian American mission heritage). Yup'ik name: Akalleq.
Background: Former U.S. Rep AK-At-Large (D), Sep 13 2022 – Jan 3 2025. Won 2022 special election (Aug 16 2022) over Sarah Palin via RCV — first Alaska Native ever elected to Congress, first woman to AK's at-large House seat. Lost 2024 re-election to Nick Begich III. ON JAN 12 2026 announced 2026 U.S. Senate campaign challenging incumbent Sen. Dan Sullivan (R). Former ED Kuskokwim River Inter-Tribal Fish Commission; AK state House 1999–2009 (Bethel). Russian Orthodox / Yup'ik. Father Ward Sattler (German-American pilot/teacher); mother Elizabeth 'LizAnn' Williams (Yup'ik). Husband Eugene 'Buzzy' Peltola Jr. died Sept 12 2023 in a Piper Super Cub crash near St. Mary's, AK; 7 children (blended).
