Official Profile

Background: Rodolfo 'Rudy' Salas Jr., born March 12, 1977 in Bakersfield, CA; Mexican-American Central Valley farm-labor family ('worked his way from laboring in the fields with his father'). UCLA BA history/political science (2000). One year as VP Al Gore WH staffer; CSU Bakersfield Upward Bound counselor; district director for State Sen. Dean Florez 2004-2010. Bakersfield City Council Nov 2010 (first Latino in 112-year council history). CA Assembly AD-32 Dec 3 2012 – Dec 5 2022 (5 terms; Moderate Caucus leader; Business & Professions Committee chair until stripped April 2017 in retaliation for SB 1 gas-tax NO vote per Bakersfield Now + CA Political Review). Lost CA-22 to David Valadao 51.5%-48.5% (2022) and 53.4%-46.6% (2024). Filed FEC paperwork for 2026 3rd attempt + listed by Ballotpedia News Mar 9 2026 + endorsements page rudysalas.com/endorsements live — but DID NOT APPEAR on the June 2 2026 jungle primary ballot (CA SoS results show only Valadao, Bains, Villegas, Garcia, and one minor candidate). No public withdrawal-statement located in this research pass; CSO believed to have backed Bains instead.
WITHDREW from 2026 CA-22 D primary before 2026-06-02 ballot — DID NOT APPEAR on certified jungle-primary ballot per CA SoS results page. 2026 campaign infrastructure (rudysalas.com + endorsements list) remained live without withdrawal banner at fetch time; no public withdrawal-statement located.
