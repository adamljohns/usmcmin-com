Official Profile

Background: Brandi Gabbard. City of St. Petersburg, Florida City Council District 2 (NE St. Pete — Barcley Estates / Northeast) since January 2018; re-elected unopposed 2021; current term ends January 7, 2027 (TERM-LIMITED). Officially NONPARTISAN ballot; personal voter registration Democrat. Filed March 2026 to run for St. Petersburg MAYOR in November 2026, challenging incumbent Mayor Ken Welch — positioning to the LEFT of Welch on DEI/LGBTQ issues. Past Council Chair. Indiana native; moved to St. Petersburg 2003 with husband Keith; one son in Barcley Estates. Real-estate broker-associate at Smith & Associates (also Suncoast Realty Solutions); 18+ years in Tampa Bay, $70M+ in sales. 2014 President, Pinellas Realtor Organization; 2015 Pinellas Realtor of the Year; 2018 Chair, NAR Insurance Committee (testified before FL Governor and Cabinet on flood insurance). Religion / church affiliation not publicly stated.
