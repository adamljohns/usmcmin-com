Official Profile

Religion: No public faith profession located
Background: John Hugh 'Buddy' Dyer (b. Aug 7, 1958, Kissimmee FL). BA civil engineering Brown University; JD University of Florida College of Law. Civil engineer / attorney by training. FL State Senate 1992-2002 (Senate Minority Leader 1998-2000); 2002 Democratic nominee for FL Attorney General (lost to Charlie Crist). Mayor of Orlando since March 2003 — 48th and longest-serving mayor. Divorced Karen Caudill 2017 (2 sons); remarried Susie Pinson Galloway Shields 2021. 2005 absentee-ballot fraud charge dismissed; reinstated by Gov. Bush. Central public figure in 2016 Pulse nightclub aftermath.
