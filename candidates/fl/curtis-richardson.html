Official Profile

Religion: Christian — long-time member of Bethel AME Church Tallahassee, serving as usher and trustee (publicly listed on bethel1865.org Board of Trustees roster and confirmed on talgov.com bio).
Background: Curtis B. Richardson (b. Oct 8, 1956, Jacksonville, FL; raised Green Cove Springs). BS Psychology (FSU); MA Counseling Psychology (UWF); MS School Psychology (FSU 1983). School psychologist Gadsden County 1983-1991; staff to FL Education Commissioner Betty Castor; Deputy Director of Cabinet Affairs under Gov. Lawton Chiles; Director of School Improvement Gadsden. Leon County School Board 1990-1996 (first Black male elected, served as Chair). Florida House of Representatives District 8 (D, Tallahassee) 4 terms 2000-2008. Tallahassee City Commission Seat 2 since 2014 (re-elected 2018, re-elected Nov 2024 over Dot Inman-Johnson). Currently Mayor Pro Tem. Married to Leon County Judge Nina Ashenafi-Richardson. Long-time member of Bethel AME Tallahassee (usher / trustee).
