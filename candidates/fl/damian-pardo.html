Official Profile

Background: Financial advisor; longtime LGBTQ-rights activist. Founding Chair of SAVE (Safeguarding American Values for Everyone, aka SAVE Dade) in 1993 — the org that successfully lobbied to restore Miami-Dade's gay-rights anti-discrimination ordinance in 1998 after the 1977 Anita Bryant repeal. Co-founded 4Ward Miami (Gay8 Festival); led CareResource (AIDS service org); served on Miami-Dade Gay & Lesbian Chamber. Raised Catholic (St. Hugh + Belen Jesuit Prep); current faith identity not publicly documented. Nov 21 2023 won runoff vs. Sabina Covo; sworn in Dec 2 2023 to 4-year term ending 2027 (postponement that would have pushed term to 2028 was struck down by FL 3rd DCA Aug 2025); first openly gay person elected to Miami City Commission.
Miami City Commission District 2. [2026-06 RESOLUTE refinement evidence-pass] First openly gay Miami City Commissioner (Nov 2023).
