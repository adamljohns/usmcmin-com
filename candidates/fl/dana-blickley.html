Official Profile

Religion: Publicly professed Christian — March 2024 Space Coast Daily Women's History Month quote: 'thankful for Grace, the precious gift of life, and my relationship with Jesus Christ, through which all things are possible' (denomination/specific church not identified in public sources)
Background: Dana Blickley is the elected constitutional officer Property Appraiser of Brevard County, Florida (Republican). First elected November 2012 defeating incumbent Jim Ford by 14 points in the GOP primary; re-elected unopposed in 2016, 2020, and 2024; current term runs through January 2029. Education: BA and MA in Public Administration, University of Central Florida. Certified Florida Appraiser (CFA). 30+ years in mass appraisal / assessment administration; worked 20 years inside BCPAO (fired in 2006 by then-incumbent Ford). Brevard County resident 30+ years; married with two children; father was a Marine. Civic: board member of National Veterans Homeless Support (NVHS) since 2012; established a scholarship endowment for veterans at Eastern Florida State College. 2012 candidacy backed by the Liberty Catalyst Fund (Republican Liberty Caucus of Central East Florida + Space Coast Patriots + Brevard 9/12 grassroots coalition). Active 2025 speaking circuit: Space Coast Patriots, Merritt Island civic groups, Viera Q&A, Republican Women's Federation.
Next Election Year: 2028
