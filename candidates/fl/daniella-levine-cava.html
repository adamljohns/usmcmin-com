Official Profile

Religion: Jewish — first Jewish Mayor of Miami-Dade County (JTA Jan 2021); no public profession of Christ; no documented anti-Christian-display action either
Education: BA Psychology, Yale 1977; JD Columbia 1981; MSW Columbia 1983
Background: Mayor of Miami-Dade County, Democrat. Elected 2020 (first female Miami-Dade mayor); re-elected outright in Aug 20, 2024 nonpartisan primary at 58% (general election cancelled). Current term to Nov 21, 2028. Prior: Miami-Dade County Commissioner 2014-2020; founded Catalyst Miami (Human Services Coalition). Born Sept 14, 1955, NYC. Husband Robert Cava (physician); two children incl. one openly gay child (motivation cited for LGBTQ advocacy).
