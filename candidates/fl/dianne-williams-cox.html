Official Profile

Religion: Christian — 40-year member of Bethel Missionary Baptist Church, Tallahassee; serves on several ministries (per City of Tallahassee official bio and FAMUAN feature).
Background: Dianne Williams-Cox (b. Quincy, FL). James A. Shanks HS honors graduate 1981; BS Data Processing Technology (FAMU); MBA (Nova Southeastern). Career: software developer GM; Harris Government Systems; 19 years State of Florida; FAMU adjunct professor. Currently Direct Services Quality Assurance Manager for Voices for Florida and owner of DWC Management Consulting Services. Married 33+ years to Thomas 'Tommy' Cox; three adult sons, six grandchildren. Founder, Southside United Citizens Action Alliance; former Tallahassee-Leon County Planning Commission; ran for FL House District 8 in 2014. 2018 Capital Outlook 'Community Activist of the Year.' First elected to Tallahassee City Commission Nov 2018; re-elected outright Aug 2022. Past Vice Chair Leon County DEC; past President Capital City Democratic Women's Club; Region 1 Chair Democratic Women of Florida. 40-year member of Bethel Missionary Baptist Church Tallahassee, multiple ministries.
