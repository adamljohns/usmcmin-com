Official Profile

Background: Born Jersey City NJ; Trinity College (DC) 1975; Stetson Law J.D. 1978. Naples real-estate/banking attorney (Kelly, Passidomo & Alba), board-certified in real-estate law; past president of the Collier County Bar.
Evidence-finalized 2026-06-19. Conservative Senate President. HARD/leadership: drove and supported SB300 (2023 6-week heartbeat ban) — sought the rape/incest/trafficking exceptions; advanced HB1069 (2023) expanding the classroom-instruction ban to pre-K-8 and barring compelled pronoun use; championed HB1 (2023) universal school choice ('one of the most transformative education bills in the history of the state'); supported HB543 (2023 permitless carry), spoke for it on the floor (Senate 27-13). Immigration: SB1718 (2023, E-Verify for 25+ employees) passed the Senate party-line under her presidency. CROSS-PARTY DEVIATION (2A): voted YEA on the 2018 Parkland SB7026 (Marjory Stoneman Douglas Act — age-21 long-gun, 3-day wait, bump-stock ban, created red-flag orders; roll call lists 'Passidomo-28 Y') and as Senate President opposed lowering the gun-buying age back to 18 and let HB759 stall — scored FALSE on self_defense q1. NOTE: the Andrew Warren suspension proceeding (Aug 2022) occurred under President Wilton Simpson, not her. Christian-liberty, economics, elections, and 10th-Amendment cells returned no individual evidence and are null.
