Official Profile

Publicly states 'faith' as one of his three governing values; inauguration included a Mayor's Inaugural Prayer Breakfast (christian_liberty q0 TRUE — affirms right to publicly profess faith in office). Primary sponsor of the Jacksonville Illegal Immigration Enforcement Act (Ord. 2025-0147), passed 12-5 in spring 2025, mandating city-agency cooperation with federal/state immigration enforcement and funding JSO mobile-fingerprint readers — border_immigration q1/q2 TRUE.
