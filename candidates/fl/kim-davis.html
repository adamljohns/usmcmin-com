Official Profile

Background: Kim Davis is City Council member, City of Cape Canaveral, Florida (Democrat). NOT to be confused with Kim Davis the Rowan County KY clerk who refused same-sex marriage licenses in 2015. Florida native; one of the first babies born at Cape Canaveral Hospital; retired Brevard County Sheriff's Office deputy (served Cape Canaveral in early 1980s); former small-business owner; mother of a child with a disability. Party affiliation Democrat per Brevard County Democratic Executive Committee Elected Officials page. First elected and reelected in 2022 against Mickie Kellum + Gregory McDonald + Carroll Sanders for two seats; won. 2022 term implies term-end ~November 2025; she remains listed on city site in 2026. Civic record: Cape Canaveral Advisory Board for Culture and Leisure Services; Project Manager/Treasurer Ais Village Trail Public Archaeology Organization; former President VFW Auxiliary; Junior Vice Auxiliary Post 348 American Legion; Trustee VFW Auxiliary; Board member Space Coast Little League. Currency caveat: 2024 Cape Canaveral community FB-group post 'Councilmember Kim Davis suffers stroke' noted but city page still lists her in June 2026; no public evidence of resignation found — flagged for next sweep verification.
[2026-06 RESOLUTE LOCAL refinement — STATUS FIX] Kim Davis is NO LONGER on the Cape Canaveral City Council. Elected Nov 2021 to a 3-year term alongside Mayor Wes Morrison and Don Willis; term ran Nov 2021 — Nov 2024. The City of Cape Canaveral's authoritative Elected Officials page (capecanaveral.gov/community_/elected_officials.php) currently lists 5 members — Morrison, Jackson, King, Shoriak, Willis — and Davis is NOT among them; she was succeeded Nov 2024 by Andrea King + Tom Shoriak (the two seats up that cycle). Mechanism of departure (lost vs did-not-file) is NOT documented in surfaced public sources, so status set to 'former' rather than 'lost'. Vote-USA still lists her as 'Currently Elected' but that profile is stale; the city's own page is authoritative.
