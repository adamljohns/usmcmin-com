Background: Kim Davis is City Council member, City of Cape Canaveral, Florida (Democrat). NOT to be confused with Kim Davis the Rowan County KY clerk who refused same-sex marriage licenses in 2015. Florida native; one of the first babies born at Cape Canaveral Hospital; retired Brevard County Sheriff's Office deputy (served Cape Canaveral in early 1980s); former small-business owner; mother of a child with a disability. Party affiliation Democrat per Brevard County Democratic Executive Committee Elected Officials page. First elected and reelected in 2022 against Mickie Kellum + Gregory McDonald + Carroll Sanders for two seats; won. 2022 term implies term-end ~November 2025; she remains listed on city site in 2026.
