Official Profile

Religion: Christian
X / Twitter: @RepMaxwellFrost
FL-10 (Orlando) since 2023; youngest House member when elected (b. 1997, Afro-Cuban); former March for Our Lives national organizing director — gun control is his signature issue (platform: assault-weapons ban, magazine limits, 'dismantle the NRA'; Giffords endorsed; Everytown-endorsed gun-violence research bill). Progressive on life (Born-Alive nay 2023 + 2025, NARAL 100%), gender (HR 734 + HR 28 women's-sports nays, Equality Act cosponsor), elections (SAVE nay twice, also NO on banning noncitizen voting in DC), border (HR 2 + Laken Riley nays). Took office after the RFMA vote (12/2022) — no marriage roll call. DEVIATIONS: YEA on H.Res 894 declaring anti-Zionism antisemitism while 92 Dems voted present (Roll 697, 12/2023); Heritage Action 26% in the 118th, unusually high for a progressive; AYE on the 2023 debt-limit deal. Voted NAY on the April 2024 Israel supplemental (yea on Ukraine); listed by Track AIPAC as rejecting AIPAC support (unverified).
