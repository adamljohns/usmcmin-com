Official Profile

Background: Terrance Freeman — Jacksonville City Council At-Large Group 1, R; age ~51. Career: Executive Council Assistant to former Council Member Aaron Bowman starting 2015; subsequently Senior Project Manager / Senior Program Manager of Apprenticeship Programs at Miller Electric Company. Appointed by Gov. Rick Scott to Council District 10 in 2018; elected At-Large Group 1 in 2019; re-elected 2023 with 65% (100,297 votes — highest of any candidate on ballot). Council Vice President 2021; Council President 2022-2023. Chairs Rules Committee; serves on Finance Committee. Married, father of five. Bachelor's and master's degrees in physical education / sports psychology. Father was an Army veteran and Baptist minister; mother a career social worker. Submitted resignation May 28, 2026 to run for Florida House District 12; formally vacates council seat Nov 2, 2026; HD-12 Republican primary Aug 18, 2026 (currently unopposed); special election to fill council seat Aug 18, 2026.
