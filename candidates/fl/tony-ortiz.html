Official Profile

Background: Antonio 'Tony' Ortiz. Orlando City Commissioner, District 2 (east Orlando / Azalea Park / Curry Ford / Lee Vista), since May 1, 2008; re-elected unopposed 2023; current term ends January 10, 2028. Officially nonpartisan; personal voter registration switched from Republican to Democrat on May 6, 2025 (cited GOP positions on LGBTQ rights, voting rights, immigration, climate); reportedly considering a 2027 Orlando mayoral run. Born Manatí, Puerto Rico; Orlando resident since 1989 — first/only Puerto Rican commissioner of City of Orlando. U.S. Marine Corps 1984, 9 years active+reserve, Persian Gulf War veteran. 14 years Orlando Police Department (Special Task Forces, Gangs Unit, Uniform Drug Unit, SWAT). Accounting/banking professional. Past President, Florida League of Cities. University of Central Florida.
Orlando City Commissioner D2 since 2008 (NP ballot, personal voter reg R→D May 6, 2025); term to Jan 10, 2028; considering 2027 mayoral bid.
