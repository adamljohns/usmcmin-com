Official Profile

Religion: Member, Rush Memorial Congregational Church, Atlanta (campaign bio); daughter of civil-rights leader Rev. Joseph E. Boone
Background: Longtime southwest Atlanta community figure and former 25-year city/council staffer. District 10 council member since 2018; chairs the Public Safety Committee. Re-elected unopposed Nov 2025.
Atlanta City Council District 10; re-elected unopposed Nov 4, 2025. [2026-06 RESOLUTE refinement] HARD evidence: voted AGAINST the June 2020 ordinance to withhold $73M from APD during the defund push (failed 7-8); voted YES on the Public Safety Training Center land lease (Sept 2021, 10-4) and YES on the $67M funding (June 2023, 11-4); as Public Safety chair shepherded the $10M APD pay-raise package and backs hiring 2,000 officers — public_justice q0 TRUE.
