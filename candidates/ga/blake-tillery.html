Official Profile

Religion: Not documented
Background: GA State Senate District 19 (Vidalia/Toombs County) since 2017; chairs Senate Appropriations and the Special Committee on Eliminating Georgia's Income Tax. Strong-conservative legislator: prime sponsor of SB39 (2025 ban on state-plan gender-affirming care) and SB21 (2025 anti-sanctuary), co-sponsor of SB36 (RFRA), SB202, SB390, SB420.
Evidence-scored 2026-06-20. Consistent Georgia conservative (96% Turning Point Action lifetime, 98% Faith & Freedom); no deviations found. Life: iVoterGuide affirms 'legal protection from conception until natural death,' opposes PP funding; supported HB481 (party-line R). Marriage/sexuality: iVoterGuide one-man/one-woman; PRIME SPONSOR SB39 (state-plan gender-care ban); YEA SB140 (minor gender-care ban); co-sponsor SB390 (anti-ALA library). Family: YEA SB233 vouchers, authored pro-voucher op-ed. Christian liberty: co-sponsor SB36 (RFRA), iVoterGuide conscience-exemption support. Elections: YEA + co-sponsor SB202 (photo ID, drop-box/Zuckerbucks restrictions). Border: YEA HB1105, PRIME SPONSOR SB21 (anti-sanctuary), co-sponsor SB420 (foreign farmland). 2A: SB319 const-carry (anti-Dem-amendment vote), NRA 'A' (2016). Justice: YEA SB63 (cash bail). Several cells null (no documented position).
