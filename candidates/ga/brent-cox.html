Official Profile

Committees: Education (Vice Chair, Policy Subcommittee), Agriculture & Consumer Affairs, Transportation, Technology & Infrastructure Innovation (Sec.); Deputy Whip; co-chair Georgia Israel Caucus. Uncontested in the May 2026 GOP primary; faces Mateo Sanabria (D) in November — confirmed running.
Newer member (since 2023); scored from a mix of verified-vote-list entries and cited campaign self-statements. CPAC 92% (2024); GA Republican Assembly 'C'/59 (moderate, no specific defection found). SB 233 (vouchers), HB 1105 (immigration) individual votes NOT confirmable → null.
