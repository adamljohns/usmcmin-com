Official Profile

Religion: Christian background (former Fellowship of Christian Athletes chapter president, Tuskegee ~1970; no current church affiliation documented)
Background: State senator SD-26 (Macon/Bibb + Hancock, Twiggs, Washington, Wilkinson cos., central GA) since Jan 2013; previously GA House from Jan 1975 — 2025 marked his 50th year in the General Assembly. B.S. Tuskegee; M.D. Mercer School of Medicine. Founding member of 100 Black Men. Committees: Appropriations (Ex-Officio), Health & Human Services, Insurance & Labor, Regulated Industries & Utilities, Transportation, Urban Affairs (Sec.). Confirmed running 2026 Democratic primary; won 2024 unopposed.
Long-serving progressive Democrat; uniformly liberal voting record with documented contrary positions on life, sexuality, school choice, voter ID, cash bail, immigration enforcement, and guns. No verified conservative cross-party deviation. HARD/verified: voted AGAINST HB 481 (heartbeat) and SPONSORED SR 136 (reproductive-freedom amendment) + SB 15 (remove 'unborn child' from natural-person definition) — 'Pro-Choice Champion'; voted NO on SB 185 (inmate gender-care ban, official roll #128, listed #8 in NO column); voted AGAINST SB 233 vouchers; OPPOSED SB 202 voter-ID (noted floor speech); voted NO on HB 1105 (anti-sanctuary, official roll #719 — NOT in the 34 YES); voted AGAINST SB 319 permitless carry (party-line, all Dems opposed) and sponsored gun-control (assault-weapon waiting period, SB 53 storage).
