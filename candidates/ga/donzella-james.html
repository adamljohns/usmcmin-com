Official Profile

Religion: Unconfirmed (Wikipedia lists Catholic; not independently verified)
Background: State senator SD-28 (south Fulton/Atlanta metro). In the Georgia Senate 1995-2003, then continuously since Dec 2009 (special election to replace Kasim Reed); redistricted into SD-28. Retired federal employee; former Atlanta Public Schools teacher; radio co-host. Morris Brown College. Chair, Urban Affairs; Vice Chair, Interstate Cooperation; on Appropriations, Economic Development & Tourism, Education & Youth. GA WIN List (Democratic women's PAC) endorsed. Running 2026 Democratic primary (May 19) vs. Mel Keyton — confirmed active.
Long-serving progressive Democrat with a uniformly liberal documented record AND ONE verified cross-party deviation on guns. Contrary (HARD, verified roll calls / sponsorships): sponsored SR 136 (reproductive-freedom amendment) + SB 15 (remove 'unborn child' from natural-person definition), voted NO on HB 481 heartbeat ('Pro-Choice Champion'); NO on SB 140 (gender-transition ban, Senate Vote #192, 3/6/2023) and NO on SB 185 (inmate gender care, did NOT join the 4 Dem crossovers); NO on SB 233 vouchers (#155); NO on SB 36 RFRA (party-line); NO on HB 1053 anti-CBDC (#731, one of 11 NO); opposed SB 202 (party-line); NO on SB 63 cash bail (#75) and SB 92 prosecutor oversight (#117); NO on HB 1105 anti-sanctuary (#719); opposed SB 319 constitutional carry (party-line). DEVIATION (HARD, verified FastDemocracy roll #182): voted YES on SB 163 (firearm preemption of local gun-control ordinances, NRA-backed, 3/6/2025) — a pro-choice Democrat siding with the gun-rights position.
