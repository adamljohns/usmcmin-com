Official Profile

Background: Atlanta native; civil-rights attorney. B.A. psychology (Spelman), M.S. (Georgia State), J.D. cum laude (NCCU Law); Director, Equity Assistance Center-South, Southern Education Foundation. APS Board of Education District 6 member 2013-2024 (chair/vice-chair). Won the Dec 3, 2024 special runoff for At-Large Post 3 (60.0% over Nicole Evans Jones); sworn in Dec 16, 2024; re-elected unopposed Nov 4, 2025 (89,913 votes), term to Jan 2030.
Atlanta City Council At-Large Post 3; sworn in Dec 16, 2024 (special runoff); re-elected unopposed Nov 4, 2025. [2026-06 RESOLUTE refinement] NOT on the council 2020-2023, so the 2022 abortion resolutions and the 2021/2023 training-center votes are not hers. On the APS board she voted YES in the 5-4 KIPP charter renewal/lease vote (2018) — single renewal vote, insufficient for the 'universal school choice' cell, left null. Frames gun violence as a public-health crisis (Cure Violence) — noted, not scored. No religion/church publicly identified.
