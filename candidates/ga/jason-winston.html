Official Profile

Background: Georgia State University business degree (finance/marketing) and marketing master's; small-business owner and GSU instructor; Grant Park resident. District 1 member since Jan 2022; re-elected unopposed Nov 4, 2025 (8,071 votes). Chaired City Utilities (2023) and Community Development/Human Services (2024-25); chairs Finance/Executive (2026).
Atlanta City Council District 1; re-elected unopposed Nov 4, 2025, term to Jan 2030.
