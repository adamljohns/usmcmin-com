Official Profile

Religion: Latter-day Saint (LDS/Mormon, convert)
Background: Idaho Attorney General since 2023; U.S. Rep. ID-01 2011–2019; founding member of the House Freedom Caucus; Puerto Rico-born former immigration attorney.
Evidence-scored 2026-06. Sitting Idaho AG, running for reelection 2026 (unopposed GOP primary). Litigation-aggressive record: defended near-total abortion ban at SCOTUS (Moyle), defended HB71 trans-minor-care ban (Poe v. Labrador), defended HB710 library law + HB93 school-choice tax credit, 100+ multistate suits vs federal mandates (Title IX, vaccine mandates, EPA/WOTUS, ATF pistol-brace), co-led parole-in-place immigration suit. Congressional Heritage Action 92%. DEVIATION NOTE: in Congress he was an immigration MODERATE/reform negotiator (Gang-of-8-adjacent) before hardening — deportation cells held null. Election-integrity record thin and his marquee RCV-initiative suit LOST.
