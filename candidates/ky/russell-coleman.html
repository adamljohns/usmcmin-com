Official Profile

KY Attorney General (R), sitting since Jan 2024 (term to ~Jan 2028); former U.S. Attorney W.D. Ky. Deep cited record: defended KY's fertilization-based 'human being' definition (motion to reconsider, May 2026), SCOTUS mifepristone brief, abortion-pill civil investigation/subpoenas; co-led the multistate suit vs Biden's 2024 Title IX gender rule + joined Skrmetti amicus defending youth gender-care bans; led an 18-state coalition backing LA's Ten Commandments law + opined KY public display is constitutional; child-safety (Character.AI suit, child sex-doll-ban defense, HB 227); Violent Crime Reduction Team + emergency appeal to hold 240+ violent offenders; joined the 26-state Alien Enemies Act deportation brief. NOTE: the pre-2024 Second Amendment / Dobbs amicus briefs on the KY AG site are Cameron-era, NOT Coleman -> guns and anti-ESG left null (no confirmed Coleman-era action). His IVF carve-out (arguing the abortion ban does not reach IVF embryo disposal) is a litigation-scope nuance -> left null rather than over-read as a FALSE.
