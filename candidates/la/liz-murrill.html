Official Profile

LA Attorney General (R), first woman in the office, sitting since Jan 2024; former LA Solicitor General. Deep, uniformly conservative cited litigation record: Louisiana v. FDA (mifepristone) + first-in-nation extradition of an out-of-state abortion-pill doctor; vigorous defense of LA's minor gender-care ban + Skrmetti/Title IX work; lead defender of LA's Ten Commandments classroom law (HB 71, won 5th Cir. en banc); 23-state anti-ESG coalition; Louisiana v. EAC proof-of-citizenship suit; sought to overturn Roper (juvenile death penalty); sued Biden DHS/ICE/CDC, census-apportionment suit, defended Trump birthright-citizenship EO + challenged New Orleans sanctuary policies; sued the ATF (private-sale + Frame-or-Receiver rules) and was thanked by the NRA for defending LA constitutional carry. No contrary evidence found. Next regular AG election 2027 (no announced bid yet).
