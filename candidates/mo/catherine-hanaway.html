Official Profile

Sitting Attorney General of Missouri (45th; first woman MO AG) -- Gov. Kehoe APPOINTED her Aug 19 2025; sworn in Sep 8 2025 after Andrew Bailey resigned to become FBI Co-Deputy Director. Term runs to Jan 8 2029; she has said she intends to run for a full term in 2028 (there is NO 2026 MO AG race -- the prior 'Attorney General candidate' framing was stale). Deep prior record: first woman MO House Speaker (2003-2005), U.S. Attorney E.D. Missouri (2005-2009, 4,000+ cases). Uniformly conservative across all 10 axes with no deviations. Pro-life (defended MO parental-consent law -> Missouri Right to Life thanked her by name; challenging Amendment 3; mifepristone suits). Praised MO Supreme Court upholding the SAFE Act (gender-transition ban for minors). Backed SJR 39 religious-liberty amendment; opposed MONA. Defended MO Voter ID law; led multistate election-integrity coalitions. Career prosecutor; removed a corrupt St. Louis sheriff. First-in-the-nation suit to exclude illegal aliens from the census. Passed MO's first concealed-carry law as Speaker (NRA 'True Ally'). Multistate suits vs. EPA, census, federal abortion rules. Evidence-finalized 2026-06.
