Official Profile

Attorney General of Mississippi (40th; first woman MS AG, first Republican MS AG since 1878) since Jan 2020, re-elected 2023 (~58% over Democrat Greta Kemp Martin); former MS State Treasurer 2012-2020. LANDMARK: brought/led Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health Organization, the case that overturned Roe v. Wade (June 2022). Uniformly conservative across all 10 axes; no deviation found. Pro-life: Dobbs, EMTALA/Idaho (Moyle) amicus, mifepristone opposition. Title IX litigation vs. Biden gender-identity rule (multiple wins). Parental-rights amicus + defends MS social-media age-verification law (NetChoice v. Fitch) + pressed PornHub on minors. Led a 22-state Navy SEAL religious-liberty amicus; applauded the Coach Kennedy school-prayer decision. Anti-ESG (challenged ISS/Glass Lewis, BlackRock, UN-led bank alliance). Election integrity: Texas v. PA, Boockvar amicus, sued over federal voter-registration EO 14019. Pro-2A amici (Bruen, Snope/AR-15-ban, GOA v. Garland). Border: 287(g), supported TX border barriers, backed TdA-gang deportation. Multistate suits vs. Biden vaccine mandates. (Caution: 'Fitch Ratings' probe stories refer to the credit agency, not the AG.) Evidence-finalized 2026-06.
