ND Governor (R), took office Dec 2024 (term to 2028), confirmed governing June 2026. Prior: ND at-large U.S. Rep (2019-24), ND state senator, ND GOP chair. Mainstream-to-strong conservative by ratings (Heritage lifetime 85%, ACU ~82, NRA A+, SBA A). HARD: cosponsored Life at Conception Act; signed ND HB 1181 (biological-sex definitions) and HB 1144 (school bathroom/pronoun/parental-notification on trans); signed HB 1588 (carry/self-defense enhancement); signed historic property-tax relief (HB 1176) + used line-item vetoes for budget discipline; signed HB 1165 tightening mail-ballot deadlines. NOTABLE CROSS-PARTY DEVIATIONS: voted YEA on the Respect for Marriage Act (2022) codifying same-sex marriage (nearly censured by ND GOP); VETOED the library-content/'book ban' bill (SB 2307) as 'legislating morality through censorship'; deep bipartisan criminal-justice-reform record (EQUAL Act with Jeffries, acquitted-conduct & juvenile-justice bills with Durbin). Ambiguous: vetoed the narrow ESA voucher bill (HB 1540) while affirming support for broader school choice -> left null. CBDC/border roll calls unverified -> null.
