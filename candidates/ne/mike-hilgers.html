Official Profile

Background: 33rd Attorney General of Nebraska (since Jan 5 2023); formally launched 2026 reelection (unopposed in the May 2026 GOP primary). Prior: NE state senator (District 21, 2017-2023) and Speaker of the Legislature (2021-2023). Scored on his AG litigation/coalition record plus documented legislative leadership.
Evidence-finalized 2026-06 from a deep, uniformly conservative HARD record. As Speaker he made the LB 933 abortion trigger ban his 2022 priority bill. As AG he has led or joined dozens of cited actions: pro-life mifepristone briefs (led 21- and 23-state coalitions), anti-trans-mandate litigation (Title IX, Skrmetti, sports, bathrooms), child-online-safety/parental-rights bills and briefs, religious-liberty briefs, an extensive anti-ESG campaign (Net Zero, Vanguard coal deal, sued ISS), election-integrity actions (citizenship verification), tough-on-crime prosecutions, aggressive anti-federal-mandate litigation (ATF, EPA WOTUS, student loans), pro-Texas-border-barrier and anti-sanctuary briefs, and pro-2A ATF-rule challenges + concealed-carry reciprocity. No cross-party deviations found. Genuine nulls: CBDC, balanced-budget, individual 2017-22 roll calls (DB did not render), and numeric issue-group ratings.
