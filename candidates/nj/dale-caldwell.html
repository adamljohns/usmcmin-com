Official Profile

Religion: Christian — ordained United Methodist minister (pastor, Covenant UMC, Plainfield NJ)
Background: 4th Lt. Governor of New Jersey and 35th Secretary of State, sworn in Jan 20 2026 after the Sherrill/Caldwell Democratic ticket won the Nov 4 2025 gubernatorial election. Ordained United Methodist minister; longtime charter-school administrator (NJ Charter School Administrator of the Year, 2015) and 26-year member of the New Brunswick Board of Education. (Prior record had no 'mayor of Fanwood' — that premise was unsupported and is corrected.)
Evidence-finalized 2026-06. CANDIDACY CONFIRMED: Sherrill/Caldwell won Nov 4 2025 (~57%); Caldwell sworn in as LG + Secretary of State Jan 20 2026. Progressive-aligned Democrat: ran on a pro-choice platform (Planned Parenthood Action Fund endorsed the ticket), serves in an administration that codified statewide sanctuary policy (Immigrant Trust Act, Mar 2026), and the ticket was endorsed by gun-control groups (Everytown, Moms Demand, Giffords, Brady). NOTED CONSERVATIVE-ADJACENT CROSS-CURRENT: he is a documented charter-school founder/administrator and the ticket supports expanding charters / public-school choice — but this is PUBLIC charter/inter-district choice, NOT vouchers/ESAs (the ticket opposed vouchers and was NJEA-endorsed), so it does not meet the universal-school-choice rubric bar and is left null with this note rather than scored true. FALSE cells below are administration/ticket-level positions (he is the elected #2 and chief elections officer), each cited; no personal Caldwell statement on marriage, crime, taxes, or religious-liberty policy was found (honest nulls).
