Official Profile

Background: 18th Attorney General of Oregon (D), sworn in Dec 31, 2024; former Oregon House Speaker (2022-2024), House member for Corvallis 2015-2024. One of the most active anti-Trump-administration AGs (48+ multistate suits).
Evidence-finalized 2026-06. Uniformly progressive AG: drove HB 2002 (2023) expanding abortion + gender-affirming care; defends mifepristone; sued to block Planned Parenthood defunding; PP-Oregon + NARAL endorsements. Leads multistate suit defending youth gender-affirming care vs HHS. Defended Oregon abortion-coverage mandate against Oregon Right to Life religious-exemption claim (appealing). Opposed SAVE Act + sued federal citizenship-verification/mail-voting EO. Defends Oregon sanctuary law; sued Trump birthright-citizenship EO (won at 9th Cir). Defended Measure 114 mag-ban/permit; Giffords-endorsed.
