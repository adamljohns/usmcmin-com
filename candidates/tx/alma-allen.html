Official Profile

Democrat, HD131 (SW Houston); 11-term incumbent, longtime public educator/former SBOE member. RETIRING — did not file for the 3/3/2026 primary; seat open (Staci Childs won the D runoff). Documented progressive record: pro-equality (Houston LGBTQ+ Political Caucus endorsement); intended NO on SB14 (House Journal statement of vote); voted NO on SB2 vouchers; joined the 2021 quorum walkout against SB1; opposed SB4 (2017). NOTE: a JBS 'Freedom Index' that appears to show conservative votes is a recorded-vote/framing artifact (e.g., her SB14 'Yea' was formally disavowed) — not scored.
