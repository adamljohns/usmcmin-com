Official Profile

TX House HD-146 (Houston), Harris County. Freshman — defeated incumbent Shawn Thierry in the 2024 Democratic runoff, took office Jan 2025; ONLY the 89th (2025) session, so 2021/2023 votes are N/A. Ran UNOPPOSED in the March 3, 2026 Democratic primary; advances to Nov 3, 2026 general. House Democratic Caucus Freshman of the Year; union organizer background (CWA shop steward). Self-identifies as queer. Pro-choice (direct quote: 'Abortion is a health care procedure'); endorsed by Planned Parenthood Texas Votes, Avow Texas, Annie's List, LPAC. Endorsed by Equality Texas c4, HRC, LGBTQ Victory Fund, Houston LGBTQ+ Political Caucus; ran explicitly against Thierry's trans-care/sports votes. In the Democratic 'no' bloc on SB2 vouchers (2025). TRTL/TAL 2025: 42% (aggregate health-bill artifact, NOT a pro-life vote — not credited). SB10 was a 'mostly' party-line vote, not unanimous, and her individual row was not confirmed — left null.
