Official Profile

TX HD-31 (South Texas), Republican since Nov 2021 party switch (was Rice's 'most conservative Democrat'); in office since 2003, running 2026 vs JJ Dominguez (D). Was the ONLY House Democrat to vote for SB8 (2021 Heartbeat Act). Texas Alliance for Life 97% (2025), TRTL 79. Voted YES SB14 (2023 minor-transition ban, confirmed both readings) and SB2 (2025 voucher). Joint author of HB1927 (2021 constitutional carry) + conference committee; NRA Defender of Freedom Award (2023), NRA/TSRA endorsed; voted AGAINST post-Uvalde rifle-age increase. Authored border-security HB7 (2023), chaired Homeland Security & Public Safety. Cited opposition to 'defund the police' as a reason for switching. Fiscal record weak by GOP standards (TFR 26/100). Researched 2026-06-19.
