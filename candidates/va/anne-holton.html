Official Profile

Religion: Roman Catholic — congregant of St. Elizabeth Catholic Church in Richmond, a predominantly African American parish; married Tim Kaine in November 1984 at a Roman Catholic church in Richmond (per Wikipedia, primary biography sources).
Education: Princeton University, Woodrow Wilson School of Public and International Affairs, magna cum laude (1980); J.D. Harvard Law School (1983).
Background: Born Feb 1 1958 in Roanoke, VA. Daughter of moderate-Republican Gov. Linwood Holton (1970-1974) — at age 12 her father enrolled her at the predominantly Black Mosby Middle School (now MLK Jr. Middle School) to end Massive Resistance to school integration, a defining biographical fact she cites constantly. Career: law clerk for Judge Robert R. Merhige Jr. (EDVA); Central Virginia Legal Aid Society attorney (1983-1998); Richmond Juvenile & Domestic Relations District Court judge (sworn 6/30/1998, chief judge 2000-2003, resigned late 2005); First Lady of VA 2006-2010 (married Sen. Tim Kaine); Virginia Secretary of Education 1/11/2014 – 7/25/2016 under Gov. Terry McAuliffe; Virginia Board of Education member Feb 2017 – ~2024 (appointed by McAuliffe); George Mason University visiting professor (May 2017) and interim president 6/20/2019 – 6/30/2020. Three adult children — Nat (b.1990), Woody (b.1992), Annella (b.1995) — all RPS graduates. Brothers Woody Holton (historian, USC) and Dwight Holton (former US Attorney, Oregon). RPS School Board, District 6 (officially nonpartisan ballot, real-world Democratic alignment) — interim-appointed Feb 3 2026 by a 7-0-1 board vote to fill the seat vacated by resigned Shonda Harris-Muhammed (Dec 2025, departed to be Southampton County superintendent); announced she will run in the Nov 2026 special election (candidacy_status set general_candidate).
