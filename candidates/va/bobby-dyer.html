Official Profile

Background: Mayor of City of Virginia Beach (officially nonpartisan; R-affiliated/Republican-endorsed; Youngkin endorsed his 2024 re-election). Born ~1949 (~76-77 yo). USMC veteran 1968-72. Physical therapist 40+ yrs in homecare. PhD organizational leadership, Regent University 2001; former asst professor of government at Regent. Won Centerville District Council seat 2004 (14 yrs pre-Mayor service); sworn in as Mayor Nov 20 2018 in special election after Will Sessoms resigned. Re-elected Nov 5 2024 with 40.9% in 5-way race (vs Wooten/Moss/Taylor/Kowalewitch); current 4-year term ends Dec 31 2028. Christian faith publicly professed in Regent alumni interview ('the good Lord had his hand on my shoulder'); specific denomination/home church not confirmed in sources reviewed.
