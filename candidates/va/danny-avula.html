Official Profile

Religion: Evangelical Christian — founding member of East End Fellowship, a multi-ethnic evangelical church in Church Hill, Richmond (NOT Catholic). Public bio at https://www.theologicalhorizons.org/events1/dannyavula confirms. Richmonder.org (campaign) flagged his 'Christian faith and affiliation with a church that's taken conservative stances on marriage and abortion' as a campaign issue — which Avula countered by publicly affirming LGBTQ equality and abortion rights.
Education: B.A. Biology, University of Virginia (1999); M.D., Virginia Commonwealth University; M.P.H., Johns Hopkins.
Background: Born 1978 in Hyderabad, India; immigrated as a child — first Asian-American and first immigrant Mayor of Richmond. Pediatrician by training. Career: Director, Richmond/Henrico Health Districts (6 yrs); Virginia State Vaccination Coordinator (Jan 2021 – early 2022, appointed by Gov. Northam); Commissioner, Virginia Department of Social Services (under Northam and Youngkin). Sworn in as 81st Mayor of Richmond Jan 1 2025; current term ends Jan 1 2029. Wife Mary Kay (RPS elementary teacher); five children including one adopted; openly gay daughter (featured in 2024 campaign ad). Self-described 'committed Democrat' on an officially-nonpartisan Richmond mayoral ballot.
