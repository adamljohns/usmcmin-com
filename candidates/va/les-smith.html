Official Profile

Education: B.S. Business Administration, St. Paul's College (1983); M.S. Human Resources Management, University of Richmond
Background: Lessie 'Les' Smith Jr.; native Chesapeake; retired Lieutenant, Chesapeake Sheriff's Office (28 years); self-employed in insurance industry; founder of Ground Zero Development Program (nonprofit tutoring young men in Chesapeake). Former Chair, Chesapeake Democratic Committee (currently Chair of its Elections Committee). 4th run for council (lost 2018, 2020, 2022); elected At-Large Nov 5, 2024; took office Jan 1, 2025; term ends Dec 31, 2028.
