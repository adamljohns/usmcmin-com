Official Profile

Religion: Catholic — inferred (not self-declared) from his 2010-2017 service on the Board of Directors of St. Mary's Catholic Cemetery, Norfolk (per professional bio https://petedecker.com/martin-thomas-jr/).
Education: B.A. Public & Urban Affairs, Virginia Tech (2002); J.D., University of Richmond School of Law (2006).
Background: Partner since 2006 at Decker, Cardon, Thomas, Weintraub & Neskis PC (Decker Law) — personal-injury, criminal, traffic, civil litigation practice. Won the Aug 23 2016 Norfolk City Council Ward 1 special election; re-elected Nov 8 2022 (79.3% vs Padraig-Eoin Leigh Dalrymple's 19.7%); current term Jan 1 2023 - Dec 31 2026. Elected Vice Mayor by council July 2018; re-elected Vice Mayor by unanimous roll-call at Jan 2023 reorganization meeting (term coterminous with Ward 1 seat). Civic record includes the Virginia College Building Authority (appointed by Gov. Ralph Northam, D) and the St. Mary's Catholic Cemetery Board (2010-2017). Wife Maggie, two sons; Wards Corner area Norfolk resident. Officially nonpartisan ballot — real-world Democratic alignment (Northam appointment + endorsed Democrat Angelia Williams Graves for VA HD-90 + appeared at McGee endorsement event with Mayor Alexander).
