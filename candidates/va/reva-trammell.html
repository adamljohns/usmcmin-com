Background: Reva Trammell — Richmond City Council District 8 (Southside Richmond). Among the longest-serving members on Richmond Council, in office since January 1, 2007 (multi-decade tenure including prior 2002-2008 service). Re-elected Nov 5, 2024 over Frank Wilson Jr.; current term ends Dec 31 2028 — NOT on the Nov 2026 ballot. Heterodox-D populist 'people's advocate' on Southside issues. The Richmond City Democratic Committee (RCDC) declined to endorse her in 2024 — she won anyway on personal brand. Known for anti-tax-hike rhetoric, vocal pro-police voice, and twice leading the (failed both times) Richmond casino referendum push despite progressive opposition.
