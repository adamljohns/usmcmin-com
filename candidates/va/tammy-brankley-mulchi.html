Official Profile

Religion: Baptist (Liberty University graduate; campaign cites faith as 'a top priority,' denomination not self-stated)
Background: VA State Senator, District 9 (Southside VA — Mecklenburg / Charlotte / Halifax region), in office since Jan 17, 2024 (won the January 2024 special election succeeding Frank Ruff). Realtor/residential contractor; ~20 years as legislative aide to Sen. Ruff and Del. Wright; former Clarksville Town Council member; founder of The Windsong Foundation (domestic-abuse victim support). Next full term on the ballot 2027.
VA State Senator, SD-9 (sitting since Jan 2024 special election). [2026-06 RESOLUTE refinement, state tier] Consistent conservative HARD record (richest roll call of this batch, via Richmond Sunlight mirror of the official feed): pro-life (voted NO on SJ247/HJ1 2025 and HJ1/SJ1 2026 reproductive-freedom amendments, plus SB754, SB743; ReproRising 2025 grade F); held the marriage line (voted NO on the same-sex-marriage amendment in BOTH 2025 and 2026 — not among the GOP crossovers); affirms biological sex (chief patron of SB723/SB749 women's-sports bills and SJ49 'affirming biological differences between the sexes'); tough-on-crime victim/penalty sponsorships (SB673 stalking, SB700 repeat prostitution-solicitation penalties, SB616/SB653 protective orders, SB844 sex-offender registry) plus SB1420 (forfeited assets for law-enforcement use); strongly pro-2A (voted NO across the entire 2025-26 gun-control slate — assault-firearms ban SB1181, magazine limits, waiting period, mandatory storage — and YES on the SB1389 safety-device tax credit).
