Official Profile

Background: WV Commissioner of Agriculture since 2017 (re-elected 2020, 2024; term to Jan 2029 — third/final term). Prior WV State Senate 2014-2017; retired USMC Lt. Colonel.
Evidence-state finalization 2026-06-17. Sitting WV Ag Commissioner since 2017 (verified Ballotpedia/WV Watch). Scored on named issue-group ratings from his WV Senate tenure (HARD per methodology): West Virginians for Life 100% (2013-2016), WV Citizens Defense League 100% (2016), American Conservative Union 83% (2016); plus cosponsorship of SB 487 (2015 WV Freedom of Conscience Protection Act). 2A cell anchored on the WVCDL 100% rating in the constitutional-carry era (individual SB 347 roll call not independently confirmed — rating is the anchor).
