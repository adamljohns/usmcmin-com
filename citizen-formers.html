Former Officials & Failed Candidates

Records preserved for officials who have left active government service — resigned, removed, retired, or moved to private sector — and for candidates who lost their election bid. We keep their scoring intact so that if they return to public office in the future, the page is ready and the historical record is documented. Methodology →

Why we keep these records: Government personnel rotate frequently — today's "former" appointee can be tomorrow's nominee. Preserving the scoring + evidence base means we don't recreate every record from scratch when someone is named to a new position. It also serves as a public-record audit trail.

Eventually monthly updates: A local-LLM persistent agent on Adam's fleet will sweep this list against current public records monthly, flag candidates who have moved back into active office, refresh the score where new evidence is available, and add new "former" records as new officials leave service.

Spotted an error? Each profile page has a dispute form. Misclassified active/former, wrong office details, or stale scoring — please submit a correction.
