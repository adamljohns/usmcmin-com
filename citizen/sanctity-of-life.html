Sanctity of Life

Personhood from conception. No carve-outs.

5 questions × 2 pts = 10 max · How scoring works · Back to scorecard

1. Sanctity of Life 2. Biblical Marriage 3. Family & Child Sovereignty 4. Christian Liberty 5. Economic Stewardship 6. Election Integrity 7. Border & Immigration 8. Self-Defense & 2A 9. Foreign Policy Restraint 10. Industry Capture & Sovereignty

What this measures

Does this candidate affirm full personhood from conception and oppose abortion, infanticide, euthanasia, and embryonic experimentation?

Why it matters

The line between civilization and barbarism is drawn at the womb. A regime that permits the deliberate killing of pre-born image-bearers (Genesis 1:27) has abdicated the first duty of righteous government (Romans 13:4). Every other category in this scorecard is downstream of this one — a candidate who cannot protect the unborn cannot be trusted with any lesser jurisdiction.

For You formed my inward parts; You wove me in my mother's womb. I will give thanks to You, for I am fearfully and wonderfully made.

— Psalm 139:13–16

The 5 scored questions

Each question is binary (True / False / null). True earns +2 points. Null is "not yet verified from primary sources" — neither penalized nor credited.

  1. Q1. Candidate affirms life begins at conception and personhood from conception
  2. Q2. Candidate has voted for or actively advocates abortion abolition (not merely restrictions)
  3. Q3. Candidate opposes embryonic stem-cell research, IVF embryo discard, and chimeric experimentation
  4. Q4. Candidate opposes euthanasia, physician-assisted suicide, and quality-of-life rationing
  5. Q5. Candidate has never accepted Planned Parenthood, NARAL, EMILY's List, or abortion-industry PAC funding

Key bills, votes, and laws we track

  • Hyde Amendment — Annual rider banning federal funding of elective abortion. We track every vote since 1976.
  • Born-Alive Abortion Survivors Protection Act — Requires medical care for infants who survive attempted abortion. Most recent votes 2019, 2023, 2025.
  • Pain-Capable Unborn Child Protection Act — 20-week federal abortion limit. Candidate support/opposition fully scored.
  • Life at Conception Act — Federal legislative recognition of personhood from fertilization. Co-sponsors get full credit.

Organizations and PAC vectors we score

  • Planned Parenthood Action Fund / NARAL — Donations or endorsements from these PACs zero out this category.
  • National Right to Life — Endorsements and grading factored as positive evidence (verify by primary source — NRLC has softened on incrementalism).
  • Susan B. Anthony Pro-Life America — Endorsements and ratings factored as positive evidence.
  • Operation Save America / Free the States — Abolitionist orgs whose endorsements indicate the strongest position.

⚠️ Position-level disqualifiers

  • Voted for any expansion of abortion access (state or federal)
  • Affirms "Roe should be restored" or "abortion is healthcare"
  • Took identifiable donations from Planned Parenthood / NARAL / EMILY's List PACs

A candidate matching any of the above is graded down to the floor of this category regardless of other answers in the rubric.
