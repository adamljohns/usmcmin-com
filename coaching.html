FAQ

Common Questions

Is this licensed therapy or professional counseling?

No. Adam is an ordained minister providing pastoral coaching and discipleship — not a licensed therapist. If you need clinical mental health support, we'll help you find the right resource. Our focus is biblical, practical, and relational.

What if I can't afford coaching?

Ministry comes first. If money is a barrier, reach out anyway. We'll work something out. No one gets turned away for financial reasons.

Is this only for Marines or military?

Not at all. USMC stands for "Uniting, Serving, Mentoring, Counseling" — our ministry pillars. All men are welcome regardless of background.

How do I cancel Warriors Circle?

Cancel anytime — no contracts, no hoops. One email or message and you're done. We want brothers, not hostages.

Do you offer group sessions or retreats?

Yes — group coaching is part of the Warriors Circle. Retreat planning is coming in 2026-2027. Stay tuned via the Field Brief newsletter.
