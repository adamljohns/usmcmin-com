What does “AI/Agentic Level” and “MCP” actually mean? (open if these are new)
AI / Agentic Level
How autonomously the tool can act on your behalf. Highest = a true agent that plans multi-step work, executes it, and recovers from errors (think Cowork, ChatGPT Agent, Claude Code). Very High / High = strong AI assistance with you still in the loop. Medium / Low = AI features inside a tool, but mostly single-shot or single-task. N/A = a model or library, not a tool you “use” agentically.
MCP — Model Context Protocol
An open standard from Anthropic (now adopted across the industry, including OpenAI and Microsoft) that lets AI agents plug into your tools, files, and other systems through a common “language.” Native = the tool was built around MCP. Yes = the tool supports MCP officially. Plugin = community plugins or third-party connectors give MCP support. No = no MCP support today. Why captains care: when MCP is supported, you can wire that tool into Cowork or Claude Desktop and ask Claude to use it on your behalf.

The use-case sentence: when you should reach for this tool specifically. Different from Captain’s Call, which is the buy/skip recommendation.

Claude ↗

General-purpose AI assistant + agentic Cowork mode

Anthropic's general AI assistant.

AgenticVery High MCPNative
Strong at writing, reasoning, and code. Long context window. Cowork mode runs agentic workflows on your desktop / mobile — the differentiator most other chat apps don't have.
Free tier · Pro $20/mo · Max from $100/mo
Best for: any captain who can only afford one AI subscription and needs writing + reasoning + agentic capability all in one.
Best default for most men. If you only buy one, this is the one.

General-purpose AI assistant + computer-use Agent

OpenAI's assistant. Largest install base.

AgenticVery High MCPYes
GPT-5 family models, image generation built into the chat, custom GPTs marketplace, voice mode. The default for many guys who tried AI first. ChatGPT Agent (formerly Operator) handles computer-use tasks.
Free tier · Plus $20/mo · Pro $200/mo · Business / Enterprise scales up
Redundancy check — Heavily overlaps Claude for general writing & reasoning. Pick one as primary; second is optional cross-check.

Captain’s Redlines

A captain decides what enters his ship. Most AI conversations are fine; some content does not belong in a cloud LLM at all. Treat these as redlines — not soft suggestions.

Pastoral / Counseling Notes
Never paste. Confidential conversations between you and a brother or congregant belong on paper, in encrypted notes, or in a counseling-specific HIPAA-aligned tool — not in Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini.
Children’s Health & Records
Never paste. Diagnoses, therapy notes, medication regimens, behavioral assessments — cloud LLMs are not the place. Use your pediatrician’s portal or a local AI (see Sovereign Stack page).
Financial Account Detail
Never paste. Account numbers, routing numbers, balances tied to identifiers, brokerage holdings with your name attached. Aggregate questions (“how do I think about a Roth conversion?”) are fine; specifics are not.
Marriage Confidences
Never paste verbatim. Specific quotes from your wife, intimate context, sexual detail, or anything she has not consented to be discussed elsewhere. Talk to her, your pastor, or a counselor — not Claude.
Identity & Security Credentials
Never paste. SSNs, passport numbers, driver’s license, API keys, passwords. Even if the tool “promises” not to train on it, the credential is now in someone else’s logs.
Other People’s Private Info
Never paste. A friend’s health, a brother’s financial struggle, a co-worker’s HR complaint. You can ask Claude how to handle a hard situation — you cannot put the other party’s name and detail into it.

The rule of thumb: if losing the conversation through a breach, subpoena, or training pipeline would be a serious problem, it doesn’t belong in a cloud LLM. When in doubt, abstract it (“a man in my counseling load…”) or move it to a local model on the Sovereign Stack.

Captain’s Stacks

You don’t need all 49. Pick the stack that fits your season — and don’t pay for the next tier until the current one is the bottleneck.

MINIMUM VIABLE

$0 / mo

For the captain who just wants to start

  • Claude — free tier, daily message limits
  • Apple Voice Memos / Notes — built into iPhone (iOS 18+)
  • Perplexity — free; the comet AI browser comes with Pro if you ever upgrade
  • NotebookLM — free; killer for research
  • Suno — free tier covers casual music gen
For: men who want to see what AI does before they pay for anything.
SOLO FOUNDER

~$40–60 / mo

For the captain running a business or ministry

  • Claude Pro $20 — primary AI + Cowork agent
  • ChatGPT Plus $20 — image gen + ChatGPT Agent for hands-off tasks
  • Otter.ai Pro $17 — meeting transcription
  • Optional: Zapier free, or Lovable free for the occasional landing page
For: most men in the bootcamp. The 80/20 stack.
POWER USER

~$150–200 / mo

For the captain leveraging AI heavily

  • Claude Max $100 — Cowork-heavy + Claude Code
  • ChatGPT Plus $20 — alt model + Agent
  • Midjourney Standard $30 — visuals
  • ElevenLabs Creator $22 — voice
  • Descript Creator $35 — podcast / video
For: captains who already ship 5+ hrs/wk of content.
