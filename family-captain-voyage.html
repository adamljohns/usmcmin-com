Captain’s Field Workbook

Your workbook, built right in. Two lines per task — what you did and what shipped — plus a weekly Captain’s Log, all saved on this device, right here in the Chart. Export any time to Apple Notes, Evernote, or your files. Prefer pen and paper, or want it in Notability? Download the fillable Field Workbook.

✎

Work on it in your notes app

The Field Workbook is fillable. Take it into a notes app and type or handwrite your answers right on the page — it saves in the file, so your work stays with you.

↓ Download the Field Workbook (PDF)

Type right into each box in Preview, Books, or Acrobat (works on any Mac/PC). Want different fonts, bold/italic/underline, or blue vs. black text? Open it in Acrobat, Apple Books, Notability, GoodNotes, or PDF Expert and use their Text tool — that formatting comes from the app. Your work saves in the file.
