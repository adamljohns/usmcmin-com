About This Credential

The NASM Certified Nutrition Coach (CNC) is an evidence-based nutrition coaching credential. CNC-certified coaches guide clients using proven nutrition strategies to support performance, recovery, and long-term health.

The CNC is not a Registered Dietitian (RD) designation — it is a coaching credential focused on behavioral nutrition, macronutrient planning, and lifestyle nutrition strategy.

Key curriculum areas: energy balance, macronutrient programming, behavioral nutrition psychology, habit formation, supplement literacy, and nutrition for performance and recovery.
