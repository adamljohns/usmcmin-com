About This Credential

The NASM Certified Personal Trainer (CPT) is the gold standard in personal training credentials. NCCA-accredited and recognized globally, the CPT is the most widely held — and most rigorously verified — personal training certification in existence.

NASM trainers are trained in the OPT Model, a science-backed periodization system that progressively builds clients from stabilization through strength to power. The curriculum covers anatomy, physiology, assessment, program design, nutrition basics, and behavior change.

Prerequisites: High school diploma · CPR/AED certification

Accreditation: NCCA — the highest standard for health and fitness certifications
