About This Credential

fit20 is a science-backed training methodology built on Slow-Motion High Intensity Resistance Training (SMHIRT). Founded in the Netherlands and now operating in 160+ studios across 12 countries, fit20 has been validated by independent scientific research.

The methodology is built on super-slow repetition tempo (typically 10 seconds up / 10 seconds down) and time under tension — pushing muscles to momentary failure without explosive movement, minimizing injury risk while maximizing muscular stimulus.

fit20 is designed for everyone: busy professionals, seniors, athletes, and complete beginners. Because sessions are private and intensity is precisely calibrated, there is no "too old" or "too out of shape" to start.

The premise: Less time. Maximum stimulus. Scientifically verified results. No excuses.
