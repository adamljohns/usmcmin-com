What This Certification Means For You

Performance isn't just for professional athletes. Speed, power, agility, and resilience are trainable qualities — and the PES specialization gives Coach Adam the tools to develop them at every level.

Coach Adam — Direct to You

"Whether you're a competitive athlete or just want to move like one — speed, power, agility, and resilience are trainable. The PES credential means Coach Adam can build programming that elevates performance, not just aesthetics."

The PES bridges personal training and athletic coaching. If you want to move better, be faster, jump higher, and perform under pressure — this is the framework Coach Adam uses.

About This Credential

The NASM Performance Enhancement Specialization (PES) is an advanced athletic performance credential used by coaches across every major professional sport. 100% of NFL teams, 100% of NBA teams, and 100% of MLB teams employ coaches with NASM PES credentials.

The PES curriculum focuses on speed development, agility training, power output, sport-specific conditioning, plyometric progressions, and performance-focused program design.

Accreditation: NCCA-accredited — the same standard applied to the CPT
