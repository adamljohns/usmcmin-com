National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM)

What This Certification Means For You

Fat loss is one of the most misunderstood goals in fitness. The WLS specialization means Coach Adam understands what the scale doesn't tell you — and how to actually move the needle sustainably.

Coach Adam — Direct to You

"Fat loss is a caloric equation, but sustainable weight loss is a systems problem. The WLS specialization equips Coach Adam to address the hormonal, behavioral, and programming factors that actually move the needle."

This isn't about crash diets or punishing cardio. WLS-trained coaches design programs that create fat loss while preserving muscle mass — the only kind of weight loss that actually improves body composition and long-term health.

About This Credential

The NASM Weight Loss Specialization (WLS) is an advanced specialization built directly on the CPT foundation. WLS-certified coaches understand the full physiological picture of fat loss — it goes far beyond "eat less, move more."

Curriculum covers: energy balance and deficit calculation, hormonal factors (cortisol, insulin, leptin, ghrelin), behavioral psychology of eating, exercise programming for fat loss without muscle loss, and plateau-busting strategies.

The WLS gives Coach Adam the tools to address why fat loss stalls, why stress and sleep matter as much as diet, and why most people fail despite doing the "right" things.

Coach Adam's Certificate

The WLS is the specialization Coach Adam most applies in practice — the majority of his clients come with fat loss as their primary goal.
