🚭 No Smoking or Vaping

Inside or on the porches. Smoking on the property is grounds for forfeiture of the full security deposit plus deep-cleaning costs.

🐾 No Pets

None of these homes are pet-friendly. Service animals as defined by the ADA are welcome with prior notice. ESAs are not service animals under federal law.

👤 Occupancy Limit Enforced

The "up to N" count on each property is the max overnight occupancy. Day-time visitors welcome — sustained over-occupancy is grounds for cancellation.

🎉 No Parties or Events

These are family homes in a residential historic district. No parties, no commercial events, no gatherings beyond the listed occupancy. The neighbors will tell us, and we will end the stay.

📹 Exterior Video Doorbells

Each property has a Google Nest video doorbell at the entry only — for safety, package monitoring, and check-in coordination. There are no cameras inside the home.

🔒 Booking Guest Must Be 25+

The reserving guest must be at least 25 years of age and present for the duration of the stay. Younger guests welcome under their oversight.

🛡 Insurance & Liability

Your personal belongings are not insured by Bow & Arrow Studio LLC. Bring renter's or traveler's insurance for valuables. The home is insured separately by the landlord.

❓ Questions Welcome

Special-circumstance request? Service-dog accommodation? Mobility considerations? Reach out before booking and we'll work with you in writing.
