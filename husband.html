HAPPY Husband

Lead your marriage like Christ leads the Church.

A biblical framework for husbands who want to lead their homes with love, wisdom, and conviction. Built on Scripture, tested in the trenches of real marriage.

HAPPY Husband FULFILLED Father RESOLUTE Citizen
H

Honors

Do you honor your wife as a co-heir of the grace of life? Honor is not a feeling — it is a decision to esteem her above yourself in word and action, even when it costs you. A man who dishonors his wife has his prayers hindered (1 Peter 3:7).

A

Abiding

Are you abiding in Christ daily? You cannot lead her spiritually if you are not being led yourself. Daily time in the Word, prayer, and repentance are not optional for a husband — they are the foundation of your authority. Apart from Him, you can do nothing (John 15:5).

P

Protecting

Are you protecting your home against spiritual, physical, and cultural threats? This means controlling what enters your household — media, influences, relationships. You are the watchman on the wall (Ezekiel 33:7). Protection is not passive — it requires vigilance.

P

Providing

Are you providing for your household? A man who does not provide has denied the faith (1 Timothy 5:8). Provision is not just financial — it is emotional, spiritual, and directional. Lead family devotions. Make the hard decisions. Set the vision for your family's future.

Y

Yields

Do you yield to God's design for marriage? Marriage is a covenant under God's authority. The husband leads, the wife submits, and both serve Christ. Yielding to this design is not oppression — it is the path to peace, unity, and joy in the home (Ephesians 5:22-33).

"Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church, and gave himself for it."
Ephesians 5:25 (KJV)
