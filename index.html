About Adam Johns

Christ-Following Husband. Father. Minister. Marine.

Christ-following husband, father, minister, and Marine — Adam Johns has lived every battle this material addresses. A 21-year USMC veteran, combat veteran, military pilot, and ordained minister, he's the husband who fought for his marriage, the father raising warriors, and the minister equipping other men to do the same.

Through U.S.M.C. Ministries, Adam brings biblical truth, military discipline, and genuine brotherhood to men who are done with mediocrity.
