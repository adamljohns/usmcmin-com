Texas Border Security & Operation Lone Star

State of Texas

Background

Operation Lone Star launched in March 2021 under Governor Greg Abbott to combat the surge of illegal immigration and drug trafficking at the southern border. Texas has spent over $11 billion on the operation.

Key Actions

  • National Guard deployment along the Rio Grande
  • Razor wire installation along border sections
  • Busing migrants to sanctuary cities (DC, New York, Chicago, Denver)
  • SB4 — state law allowing Texas law enforcement to arrest illegal border crossers
  • Buoy barrier in the Rio Grande (legal challenges ongoing)

Reformed Christian Analysis

Scripture teaches that nations have borders and that lawful authority exists to protect citizens. Texas is acting where the federal government has abdicated its duty. Christians should support magistrates who enforce the law and protect their communities from the consequences of illegal immigration — fentanyl, human trafficking, and the strain on public resources.
