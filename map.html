Virginia at a glance

Interactive map of Virginia's state outline, all 95 counties + 38 independent cities, and the 11 U.S. House districts. Hover any feature for its name; click to see who represents it on the RESOLUTE Citizen scorecard. Pass ?slug=<candidate> to land on a profile-deep-linked view that pre-highlights that candidate's district.

Source data: state outline from glynnbird/usstatesgeojson (CC0); county polygons (134 features = 95 counties + 38 independent cities + Falls Church + Manassas + Manassas Park, sourced from a US Census 2010 5m TIGER simplification); congressional district polygons sourced from the same Census 2010 atlas. State legislative district shapes will land in a future build (the Census ZCTA → SLDU/SLDL crosswalk Adam asked about in Ship 18).

Heads-up: VA's redistricting amendment passed at the polls on April 21, 2026 (51.59% Yes). A judge enjoined certification on April 22 and the Attorney General is appealing. If the new map is ultimately certified, this page will get a 2026-era CD layer added alongside the 2010 layer for comparison.
