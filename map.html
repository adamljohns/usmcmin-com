Maps

Pick a state at a glance

Detailed interactive map: state outline, all counties + independent cities, U.S. House districts, and major city centroids. Hover any feature for its name; click a city dot or district to zoom + open a popup of officials we cover. Pass ?slug=<candidate> to land on a profile-deep-linked view.

State outline County / independent city U.S. House district Major city Highlighted feature
What you see, and what's next.

Source data: state outlines from a CC0 release of the Census 2010 5m TIGER simplification. County polygons cover all counties + independent cities. Congressional district polygons reflect 2010-era boundaries (the most reliably-fetchable static set). City centroids are pulled from a US-cities-with-coordinates dataset, filtered to the cities we have in data/places.json. State-legislative district shapes are not yet shipped — that's the next ingestion. Want a state I haven't built yet? Adding a new state needs three geojson files in data/maps: <st>-counties.geojson, <st>-cds-2010.geojson, and <st>-cities.geojson.
