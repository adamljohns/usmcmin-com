Our Mission

Warriors Equipped. Kingdom Advancing.

U.S.M.C. Ministries exists to equip men to be HAPPY Husbands, FULFILLED Fathers, and RESOLUTE Citizens through biblical coaching, accountability, and discipleship. Based in Fredericksburg, Virginia.
