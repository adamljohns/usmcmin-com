RESOLUTE Citizen Tool

Petition Your Representatives

Draft a one-click letter to your U.S. Senators, House member, and Governor based on your ZIP and the issue you care about. Each template is grounded in a RESOLUTE Citizen category and cites primary sources. You control every word before you send.

1Where do you live?

ZIP lookups use data/zips.json (Census 2020 ZCTA → congressional-district crosswalk) to pin your U.S. House member. If your ZIP spans multiple districts we will show all applicable House reps. State-legislative districts are not yet resolved by ZIP — that crosswalk is the next upgrade.

2What issue are you writing about?

Category templates are drafted in plain, respectful language that a rep's intake staff can read in under 60 seconds. You can edit every word before you send.

3Personalize & review

Character count: 0

4Send to your reps

Letter tips: contact forms generally accept plain text. Keep it under 1 page. Always include your full name and city in the form fields so the staff knows you are a constituent. Following up after 1–2 weeks with a phone call gets more attention than a single email.
